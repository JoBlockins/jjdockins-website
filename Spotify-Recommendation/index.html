About This Project

This app uses the Spotify API to analyze your listening habits - like your favorite genres, artists, and recently played tracks - and then uses an AI Agent to generate a personalized daily music recommendation each day from an up-and-coming new artist you might like.

The goal is to help music listeners break out of their comfort zone and discover emerging artists that match their taste, before they blow up.

Spotify API Integration

Analyzes your listening habits, favorite genres, artists, and recently played tracks.

AI-Powered Matching

Uses an AI Agent to find up-and-coming artists that align with your music taste.

Daily Recommendations

Delivers a fresh personalized recommendation every day from a new emerging artist.
