Paper Trader

The Goal

Find NYSE/NASDAQ stocks with enough price volatility to capture a 5% gain (~$500 on a $10,000 position) within a 1–2 week swing trade. The strategy is long-only — buy near a short-term low, sell into strength.

Trade Rules

Position size: $10,000 per trade

Auto-sell ceiling: +7% gain (take profit)

Lock-in trigger: Once a position hits +5.5%, auto-sell if it dips back below that level

No stop-loss: These are quality stocks worth holding — if the swing doesn't play out, the position is held until a manual exit

This is a paper-trading project — no real money. The goal is to learn how screening, signals, and AI analysis work together to identify trade setups.

How It Works

The pipeline runs in stages, each building on the last:

1. Screener — Scans ~1,600 NYSE/NASDAQ stocks through quality filters (market cap, volume, revenue), then ranks by volatility using ATR and historical swing frequency. Only stocks that consistently show 12%+ swings in 2-week windows make the watchlist (~90 stocks).

2. Signals — Evaluates watchlist stocks with 6 weighted rules (RSI, volume spikes, moving average positioning, range position) to score each stock 0–100. Scores above 60 = BUY, 35–60 = WATCH. BUY signals are then validated by Claude Sonnet, which adds a verdict and conditional buy rule.

3. Sentinel — Before market open, fetches recent news headlines for all BUY + WATCH stocks and sends them to Claude Haiku. Flags material negatives that technical indicators can't capture.

4. Briefing — Combines signals + sentinel into one actionable view with a composite status per stock: Trade Ready, Caution, On Hold, or Watch.

5. Trading — Monitors selected stocks for buy rule conditions (via Haiku), executes paper trades automatically, and tracks open positions with sell rules (+7% ceiling, +5.5% lock-in).

When To Run

Weekend (Sunday)

Refresh the watchlist — re-run the screener to pick up new volatile stocks and drop any that no longer qualify. This sets the stock universe for the upcoming trading week.

python main.py

Daily (before market open)

Run the full pipeline: metrics → rules → AI analysis → sentinel. Check the Briefing page for which stocks to monitor and their buy rules.

python main.py --analyze
