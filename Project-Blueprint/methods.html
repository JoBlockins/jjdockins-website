Methods of Participation

Choose the format that works best for your schedule and comfort level

Flexible & Accessible

To make Project Blueprint accessible and flexible for busy professionals, there are three ways you can participate in the interview process. Each option uses the same standardized set of 50 questions, ensuring consistency while giving you the freedom to choose the format that works best for your schedule and comfort level.

In-Person Interview

Where: Your workplace, a nearby cafe, library study room, or any location you prefer

Time commitment: ~50-60 minutes

If you choose an in-person interview, I'll meet you at a location that's most convenient for you. This is the most conversational and natural option, giving us the opportunity to talk face-to-face and build a stronger personal connection.

I'll guide you through the entire set of 50 questions, ask follow-up questions when helpful, and make sure the conversation flows smoothly. During the session, I'll either take notes or-if permitted-make an audio recording so I can accurately capture your responses for the project.

Ideal for: Those who prefer real conversation and enjoy sharing their career journey in a more relaxed, interactive setting.

Zoom or Virtual Interview

Where: Online via Zoom

Time commitment: ~30-45 minutes

If meeting in person isn't convenient, a virtual interview works just as well. We'll schedule a Zoom call at a time that fits your schedule. Just like the in-person format, I lead you through all 50 questions and guide the discussion to keep it flowing.

I will record the call (with your permission) to make sure your experiences are captured accurately.

Ideal for: Busy professionals outside my local area or anyone who prefers a quick, easy online conversation.
